What We Do

Our Specialized Services

We design and construct premium outdoor living spaces and exquisite indoor hardwood floors custom-built to match your lifestyle.

Composite Decks

Virtually maintenance-free decks built using premium, weather-resistant composite materials (Trex, TimberTech). Resistance to rot, warping, and fading with a modern clean finish.

  • 25+ Year Material Warranty
  • Ultra Low Maintenance
  • Splinter & Fade Resistant

Wooden Decks

Classic natural wood decks crafted from premium Cedar, Pressure-Treated Pine, or Redwood. Offers unmatched warm, traditional aesthetics designed to fit your backyard layout.

  • Premium Selected Lumber
  • Professional Sealing & Staining
  • Traditional Warm Finish

Hardwood Flooring

Stunning solid or engineered hardwood flooring installations. Expert sanding, staining, refinishing, and precise board laying for a luxurious, durable interior upgrade.

  • Dustless Installation Available
  • Wide Selection of Wood Species
  • Custom Stains & Finishes

Extended Construction & Repair Services

Beyond new builds, A-Z Decks & Flooring offers complete renovation and supplementary construction solutions to keep your home in top shape.

Deck Repair & Structural Upgrades

Replacement of rotted framing joists, ledger board repairs, post replacement, and structurally reinforcing older decks to ensure safety code compliance.

Refinishing, Sanding & Staining

Restore the natural beauty of weathered wood decks or worn hardwood floors. Complete sanding and application of commercial-grade sealers.

Custom Pergolas & Gazebos

Add shade and elegance to your outdoor deck area. Custom designed wood and vinyl patio covers, pergolas, and privacy screens.

Custom Railings & Stairs

Upgrade old rails to modern black aluminum, cables, or glass inserts. Custom step building and integrated LED riser lighting installations.

Typical Project Pricing

Final project costs vary depending on structural prep, lumber grades, composite brands, and floor specifications. Below are general baseline ranges:

Pressure-Treated Wood Decks $20 - $35 / sq ft
Premium Cedar Decks $30 - $48 / sq ft
Composite Decking (Trex/TimberTech) $40 - $65 / sq ft
Hardwood Flooring Installation $8 - $16 / sq ft
Floor Refinishing & Sanding $4 - $7 / sq ft
